##
## This is a file that will allow us to override and add some extra information
## about our devices.  One use is to let us assign more meaningful device names
##
## Format of the file is
## MAC ADDRESS, ALTERNATE DEVICE NAME, ROOM LOCATION
##
## For example
## 000000F5FE, Strong Box and Gun Safe, Basement
##
000007AAAF,Family Room Motion,Family Room
000000B357,Garage,Garage
000000F9DE,Sliding Glass Door,Kitchen
0000093BF5,Dining Room Motion,Dining Room
